# AI Upheaval Rattles Israel's Software Industry

**Author:** Sophie Shulman
**Publication:** Ynet News

## Key Statistics

- **SaaS Index Performance (2025):** Declined 6.5% vs. S&P 500's 17.6% gain
- **Revenue Multiple Decline:** Dropped from median above 7 to below 5
- **NICE Stock Performance:** Only TA-35 index stock with negative 2025 returns
- **Next Insurance Sale:** $2.6 billion to Munich Re (2024)
- **monday.com stock drop (February 2026):** 21% amid investor concerns about agentic AI

## Major Companies Affected

Israeli software leaders experiencing significant losses include NICE, Monday.com, and Wix. These firms lost "tens of percentage points" in value during 2025 and continued struggling into 2026.

## AI Impact Details

The launch of Anthropic's Claude Code sparked industry upheaval. One documented example involved a former Amazon executive building an entire CRM system over a weekend — work traditionally requiring sophisticated enterprise platforms like NICE's offerings.

## Notable Quotes

**Dean Shahar (DTCP):**
- "A founder approaching VCs with SaaS startup won't reach pitch stage"
- "AI turned software into commodities where competitive advantage is nearly impossible."

**Lior Handelsman (Grove Ventures):**
- "SaaS faces challenges maintaining growth...barriers are falling and reaching market quickly with first product to prove feasibility is easy."

## Market Shift

Hardware companies like Tower Semiconductor and Nova have displaced traditional software firms in top Israeli company rankings. The article frames a key tension for 2025-2026: traditional SaaS work-management and CRM platforms — including Israeli champions monday.com and Wix — are increasingly threatened by agentic AI tools that allow non-developers to spin up custom CRM and workflow systems in days rather than buying off-the-shelf platforms.
